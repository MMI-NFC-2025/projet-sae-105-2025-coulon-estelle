Contact

Science, Faith and Cosmic Mystery

A film by Robert Zemeckis (1997) that tells the quest of an astrophysicist searching for extraterrestrial signals. This masterpiece of science fiction explores the boundaries between science and spirituality, and highlights a brilliant female scientist hero.

Discover Other Films

Synopsis

Dr. Ellie Arroway (Jodie Foster), an astrophysicist specializing in the search for extraterrestrial signals via the SETI program, dedicates her life to "listening" to the universe.

As the project's funding is threatened, she gains the support of a patron, S. R. Hadden, to continue her research. A few years later, Ellie detects a signal from the star Vega, containing a sequence of prime numbers — possible evidence of an intelligent civilization.

The message includes images, technical blueprints, and directs Ellie toward a mysterious machine she will use to attempt first contact.

During this journey, she experiences an intense personal encounter: a strange landscape is presented to her, she converses with an entity taking the appearance of her deceased father, then is returned to Earth. Officially, the capsule's recording shows only static noise, casting doubt on the truthfulness of her testimony.
