Related Films

Cinema and Female Representation

Cinematic works that highlight women scientists and explore themes of discovery, perseverance, and scientific recognition.

Discover Interstellar

Interstellar

2014

Where science meets emotion. Between black holes, relativity and cosmic mysteries, this film explores the limits of spacetime.

Contact

1997

Between science and spirituality, Contact tells the quest of a scientist who captures a signal from the depths of the cosmos.

The Theory of Everything

2014

This film traces Stephen Hawking's life, where the fragility of the body contrasts with the infinity of thought. Between love and science, it celebrates the power of the human spirit.

Cosmos: A Spacetime Odyssey

2014

This series transforms science into an adventure story. Driven by spectacular visuals, it takes us through time, stars and discoveries.

The Most Unknown

2018

Nine researchers explore the deepest questions of our existence. From dark matter to consciousness, this documentary reminds us that every answer opens new mysteries.

Hidden Figures

2016

Inspired by a true story, the film highlights three NASA mathematicians who remained in the shadows too long. Their genius allowed humanity to reach the stars.
